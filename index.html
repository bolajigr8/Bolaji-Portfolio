about

Hello, I am Bolaji Michael Adebayo and I am a web developer. I live in Abuja (Nyanya) and is available for work anywhere.

I spend most of my day, experimenting with HTML, CSS and JavaScript (and its endless list of frameworks). I enjoy coding and the challenge of learning something new everyday.

I am also a data analyst specialized in Excel, SPSS and Power BI. You can also find me at my Maths Youtube Channel @micboltutorials
